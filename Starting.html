Starting Location: Eldergrove

Eldergrove is a small, secluded village nestled within the vast and ancient Greenthorn Forest. The village is known for its skilled woodworkers, herbalists, and hunters. Living in harmony with nature, the residents of Eldergrove have developed a deep respect for the forest and its bountiful resources. The village is governed by a council of village elders, who are revered for their wisdom and experience.

Eldergrove is surrounded by towering trees, many of which are hundreds of years old. The village has a population of around 200, consisting mostly of humans, elves, and half-elves. The village is largely self-sufficient, but occasionally trades with travelers and merchants who brave the dense forest to visit the settlement.

Life in Eldergrove is peaceful and slow-paced, with most villagers spending their days working their craft or tending to the surrounding forest. While magic is rare in the world, the residents of Eldergrove still hold onto the belief that the spirits of the forest protect and guide them.

Key Locations:

  • The Greenthorn Inn: A cozy, welcoming inn run by a warm-hearted half-elf named Liriel. The inn is a popular gathering place for villagers, who enjoy sharing stories and a warm meal after a day of work.
  • Elder's Hall: The central meeting place for the village council and the site of important gatherings and ceremonies. The hall is a beautiful structure built around the base of a massive tree and is adorned with intricate carvings depicting the history of Eldergrove.
  • Willow's Apothecary: A small shop run by a skilled herbalist named Willow. The apothecary is well-stocked with various herbs, poultices, and remedies sourced from the Greenthorn Forest.
  • Fletcher's Workshop: A workshop run by the village's finest bowyer, Gavric Fletcher. He creates exceptional bows and arrows for the village's hunters and occasionally for trade.
  • Greenthorn Hunter's Lodge: A gathering place for the village's hunters, who spend their days tracking game and protecting the village from any potential threats. The lodge is overseen by Aelar, the village's most experienced hunter.

As you create your characters, you can use this information about Eldergrove to help shape you backstories and motivations. For example, characters might have grown up in the village, learning the skills of hunting or woodworking from their family or neighbors. Alternatively, they might be newcomers to the village, drawn to Eldergrove's quiet and peaceful way of life after a tumultuous past.
